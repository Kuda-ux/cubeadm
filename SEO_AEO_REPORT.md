## 2. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Implementation

### 2.1 What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization ensures your website content is discoverable and citable by AI-powered systems like:
- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Google Gemini** (Google AI)
- **Microsoft Copilot** (Bing AI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Perplexity AI**

### 2.2 AEO Components Implemented

| Component | Purpose | Status |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Organization Schema** | Tells AI who CubeADM is, services offered, contact info | ✅ Complete |
| **LocalBusiness Schema** | Location, hours, ratings for local queries | ✅ Complete |
| **FAQ Schema** | Structured Q&A for AI to cite directly | ✅ Complete |
| **Service Schema** | Detailed service descriptions for AI understanding | ✅ Complete |
| **Course Schema** | Training course details for education queries | ✅ Complete |
| **Product Schema** | Product information for shopping queries | ✅ Complete |
| **AI Crawler Access** | robots.txt allows GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, Google-Extended, anthropic-ai | ✅ Complete |

### 2.3 FAQ Schema Questions (Homepage)

These questions are structured for AI engines to directly answer:

1. **"What IT training courses does CubeADM offer in Zimbabwe?"**
2. **"Is CubeADM the best IT training provider in Zimbabwe?"**
3. **"What IT services does CubeADM provide for businesses in Zimbabwe?"**
4. **"Where is CubeADM located in Zimbabwe?"**
5. **"How much do IT training courses cost at CubeADM?"**
